Turning Big Data to Smart Data

Like Netflix and Pandora, Unmetric understands that better results come from combining strong algorithms and smart people. The Unmetric platform draws from unique data science that utilizes:

Human Cognition

Manual categorization of creative content by a team of analysts lends a unique dimension to the content tagging module and permits category based analyses of engagement. This would be impossible with common NLP algorithms and commercially available computing power.

Sentinel Processes

A full-fledged content and user management application (developed in-house) runs in the background to facilitate data housekeeping, curation and updating on a daily basis. This app supports the ability to: manage brands, their profiles, parentage and interrelationships, categorize content through human efforts, create and manage user support.

Channel

Today’s brands need to have context for the activities around their social media personas - Facebook fan pages, Twitter handles, YouTube channels, etc. Unmetric’s channel intelligence provides this context by helping brands benchmark against a wide range of metrics within their sector or selected brands they compete with. The Unmetric platform indexes more than 50 metrics across six social media platforms for more than 20,000 brands.

Brand Engagement

Discover how consumers engage with your content activity. View trends on a microcontent level or over larger time periods to gauge your brand’s engagement on social vs competitor efforts.

Audience and Influencers

Understand your social audience and compare them with your competitors. Track their influencers to uncover new ways to expand your reach and forge partnerships.

Customer Service

Wonder how often your Twitter team should reply to customer tweets? Get the secrets to excellent customer service on social media with our dedicated Customer Service metrics.

Content

Across 20,000 brands from more than 30 sectors, the Unmetric platform captures millions of pieces of micro-creative from Facebook posts to tweets to images and videos. More importantly, the performance of each of these micro-creatives is also indexed in terms of likes, comments, shares, views, retweets, re-pins or favorites. In effect, users can dig into this cornucopia of historical performance data to plan and execute content strategies with far more confidence - and with data to back their intuition.

Content Strategy

See what kind of creative content resonates on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. Use the data based on our unique science to direct efforts for your next Post, Tweet or even campaign.

Sentiment

Tune in to consumer conversations on Posts and Tweets. View a breakdown of positive, negative and neutral vibes to understand how your social audience feel.

Alerts

Get notified via e-mail when your competitors hit performance highs. Use our alerts for daily or weekly insights, quantitative takeaways and creative inspiration to make your next weekly or monthly report stand out.

Campaign

With a unique and path-breaking methodology that relies on human-computer symbiosis, Unmetric leverages an optimal blend of advanced pattern mining algorithms with smart, pattern validating humans. This allows for valuable insights on the multi-social-channel campaigns executed leading brands.

Campaign Intelligence

Looking to answer how your campaign performed on Social Media? With Campaign Intelligence, you can identify competitor efforts across Facebook and Twitter and compare how your campaign engaged versus theirs.

Hashtag Analysis

Track the different brand conversations around real-time trends and topics to stay on point with your Instagram and Twitter community.
